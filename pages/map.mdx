# WildHacks Event Map

## Event Spaces

WildHacks programming will take place across two buildings connected to each other by an indoor bridge.

[Learn about leaving and re-entering event spaces during the event.](/logistics/leaving-the-venue)

### Tech

Check-in, workshops, and presentations will take place in Tech. The building is accessible via the main entrance on the west side of the building, which faces Sheridan Road.

### Mudd

The **[WildHacks Help Desk](/help-desk)** and [WildSnacks Station](/logistics/food-and-drinks) will be located on the second floor of Mudd.

The area will be open 24/7 and will include [overnight spaces](/logistics/sleeping-and-showering).
